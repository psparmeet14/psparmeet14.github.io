Who I Am

I'm Parmeet Singh Bhogal, a Senior Software Engineer with 7 years of experience in building robust, scalable backend systems. My journey in software development has been driven by a passion for clean code, thoughtful architecture, and the belief that software engineering is as much about problem-solving as it is about creativity.

My Engineering Philosophy

I believe in writing code that is not just functional, but elegant and maintainable. Clean architecture, domain-driven design, and test-driven development aren't just methodologies to me—they're principles that guide how I approach every problem. I strive to build systems that are intuitive to understand, easy to extend, and a joy to work with.

Beyond the Code

Software engineering has taught me invaluable lessons about life: the importance of clarity, the power of incremental progress, and the value of continuous improvement. I'm deeply interested in how the principles we apply in our code—modularity, separation of concerns, refactoring—can also be applied to personal growth and self-development.
